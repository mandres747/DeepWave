Binaural Beats App - Your Personal Brain Entrainer

Harness the power of binaural beats and isochronic tones for better sleep, deeper focus, and true relaxation.

- 30+ scientifically-based presets in 9 categories
- Binaural beats and isochronic tones
- Solfeggio frequencies (396 Hz - 963 Hz)
- Customizable phase editor
- Waveform visualizer and frequency curve
- Breathing guide
- Background sounds (Pink Noise, Brown Noise)
- Session journal with mood tracking
- Create and save custom presets
- Session sharing via deep links
- Fully offline - no internet connection needed
- No ads, no tracking
