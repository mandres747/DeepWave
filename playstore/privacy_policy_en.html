Privacy Policy

Binaural Beats – Brain Entrainment Generator
Version 1.0 | Last updated: May 26, 2026

Summary: This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

1. Data Controller

Matthias Andres

2. Core Principle: No Data Collection

The "Binaural Beats" app does not collect, process, or transmit any personal data. The app:

  • requires no internet connection (no INTERNET permission)
  • contains no analytics or tracking tools (no Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • contains no advertisements or ad SDKs
  • does not share data with third parties
  • uses no cookies

3. Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data exclusively on your device (Android DataStore Preferences). This data never leaves your device:

Data TypePurposeDeletion
Custom PresetsFrequency templates you createdIn-app or upon uninstallation
Session JournalYour session ratings and mood notesIn-app or upon uninstallation
SettingsTheme (Light/Dark), LanguageUpon uninstallation

This data is only visible to you and is automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled.

4. Permissions

The app requests only the following permissions:

PermissionPurpose
FOREGROUND_SERVICEContinue audio playback in the background
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACKClassify background service type (Android 14+)
POST_NOTIFICATIONSShow playback status in notification bar

No permissions for internet, camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage are requested.

5. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The app does not communicate with any server.

6. Children

Since the app does not collect any data, it is safe from a data protection perspective for use by children. However, the app is primarily intended for adults.

7. Deep Link Sharing

The app offers a sharing function that generates a local link (binauralbeats://session?...) containing technical session parameters (frequencies, durations). This link contains no personal data. Sharing is performed through your device's standard share function — the app itself does not send anything.
